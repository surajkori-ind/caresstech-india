Who We Are

A young studio building STEM education you can hold in your hands.

CaressTech India is a new EdTech startup working on practical, activity-based STEM education for Indian schools, NGOs and community learning centres. We believe the fastest way to understand an idea is to build it — so every kit, worksheet and workshop we design is meant to be taken apart, wired up, coded, and tried again.

We're just getting started, and we're building CaressTech India in close collaboration with the educators and organisations we serve — which means our curriculum keeps improving with every classroom we work in.

Mission

Make hands-on STEM learning affordable and accessible for every classroom in India.

Vision

A generation of learners who build, test and iterate — not just memorise.

Philosophy

Learning by Making: concepts stick when hands are involved, not just eyes and ears.
